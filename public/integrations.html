Integration FAQs

How does Cresca OS connect with my Facebook and Google Ads?

We connect directly to your Meta and Google Ads accounts. When a lead submits a form or clicks through from any ad, Cresca OS captures their information in real time and immediately initiates a response sequence — eliminating the delay that typically kills conversion.

What happens if I use a tool that isn't on your integration list?

We can connect to virtually any platform through Zapier, Make, or custom webhooks. If you use a specialized tool for your industry, our implementation team will evaluate the best connection method during your onboarding.

Do I need to stop using my current tools to work with Cresca OS?

No. We work around your existing setup and advise on the most operationally sound path forward — whether that means a full migration to one unified system or a layered integration with your current tools. We never force a rip-and-replace approach.

Can Cresca OS receive leads from multiple ad platforms simultaneously?

Yes. Cresca OS is built to handle multi-channel lead flow from Meta, Google, TikTok, and organic web sources at the same time — routing every lead into the correct pipeline, triggering the right follow-up sequence, and logging every touchpoint automatically.
